Meet the Founder

Richard Asiamah

Richard is a Doctoral Researcher at the National Research University, Higher School of Economics in Moscow, Russia. His specialized research focuses on Urbanization and Economic Growth, utilizing advanced statistical and mathematical modeling.

Driven by a passion for technical education and impactful leadership, Richard founded The African Network, Code Ghana Youth, and Speak Ghana.

Through Code Ghana Youth, he has personally trained over 100 Ghanaian youth in programming using Python and R. His professional journey includes high-level roles at Yandex, the Institute of African Studies, and KNUST.

He leverages his expertise in Python, R, STATA, and MATLAB to mentor the next generation of African scholars, providing them with the exact architectural roadmap needed for global success.
